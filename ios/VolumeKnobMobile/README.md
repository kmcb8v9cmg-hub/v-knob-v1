# Volume Knob Mobile

This iPhone companion keeps the three-tab Volume Knob design and targets modern iPhones, including iPhone 17 Air.

Due to iOS security boundaries, it cannot capture or alter audio produced by other apps, silently record arbitrary apps, or control Apple Music globally. Its working EQ and transport controls apply to audio files opened inside Volume Knob. ReplayKit records the Volume Knob app and offers Apple's broadcast picker for user-authorized broadcasting.

Open `VolumeKnobMobile.xcodeproj` in Xcode, select an Apple Development Team, connect the iPhone, and press Run. A signed IPA requires an Apple Developer identity and provisioning profile.
